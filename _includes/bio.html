Short Bio


I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from National and Kapodistrian University of Athens in 2020.

Recently, I completed my Bachelor Thesis in Robotics and AI. I developed a Leader-Follower Scheme of two Mobile Robots using an RGB-D camera and MobileNetV2 detection system. I was fortunate enough to co-supervised by Prof. Konstantinos J. Kyriakopoulos and Prof. Ioannis Emiris. I was also a member of Control Systems Laboratory at National and Technical University of Athens (School of Mechanical Engineering) for 1.5 years.
